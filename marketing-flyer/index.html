Overview

We, The Iteration Station, are looking for willing participants to play-test our project: Aidan Interactive Rendering. Aidan Interactive Rendering will provide users with the ability to tour virtual environments that others have created. We seek to enhance the architectural landscape by offering quick renderings of different environments that are being hosted in the cloud. We will work with any person who shows an interest in our project and would help them set up and go through the progress of using the software if needed.
